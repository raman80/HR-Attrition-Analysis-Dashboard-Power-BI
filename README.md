# 📊 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to uncover key workforce trends and identify factors contributing to employee turnover. The goal is to provide actionable insights that help organizations improve retention and workforce planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition patterns
* Identify high-risk employee segments
* Understand the impact of age, salary, and job roles on attrition
* Provide data-driven recommendations to reduce attrition

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Data Modeling** – Data relationships and measures

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains employee information including:

* Employee ID
* Age
* Job Role
* Salary
* Years at Company
* Education
* Attrition Status

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 📌 KPI Cards:

  * Total Employees
  * Attrition Count
  * Attrition Rate
  * Average Salary
  * Average Age
  * Average Years at Company

* 📊 Visualizations:

  * Attrition by Age Group
  * Attrition by Education
  * Attrition by Job Role
  * Salary Distribution
  * Job Satisfaction Analysis
  * Years at Company Trend

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Higher attrition observed among employees in early career stages
* Certain job roles (e.g., Sales & Laboratory roles) show higher attrition
* Employees with lower salary ranges tend to have higher attrition
* Attrition varies significantly across age groups

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Implement targeted retention strategies for high-risk roles
* Improve compensation and benefits for lower salary groups
* Introduce employee engagement and satisfaction programs
* Focus on career growth opportunities for early-stage employees
---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee attrition trends and enables data-driven decision-making for HR teams.
